Common Go-Live Failures We Rescue

Go-live failures usually manifest in one of several predictable ways. Recognizing which type of failure you are experiencing helps us apply the right rescue methodology faster:

  • System Crashes Under Load: The system worked in testing but collapsed when 50+ users logged in simultaneously. This is usually a server infrastructure or database indexing issue.
  • Critical Data Sync Failures: Inventory counts aren't updating, or sales orders aren't generating invoices. The core relational flow of Odoo is broken. Learn about data recovery →
  • Workflow Dead Ends: Users are stuck. A validation error stops them from confirming an order, and there's no workaround. This points to poorly configured user permissions or workflow rules.
  • Integration Collapse: Your e-commerce platform (Shopify, WooCommerce) or shipping integration fails to communicate with Odoo, leaving orders in limbo.
  • Total User Paralysis: The system technically works, but the UI is so confusing or poorly customized that employees literally cannot do their jobs.

⚠️ The Golden Rule of Go-Live Rescue

Do not try to fix everything at once. The goal of emergency go-live rescue is operational stability, not perfection. We prioritize getting cash flowing, orders shipping, and invoices printing. Optimization comes later.

Our 48-Hour Emergency Triage Process

When you engage our team for an emergency go-live rescue, we don't spend weeks writing reports. We act immediately.

Hours 1-4: War Room Setup & Critical Path Identification

We get your key stakeholders on a call. We ask one question: "What processes, if they don't work today, will bankrupt the company by Friday?" Usually, this is invoicing, shipping, and payroll. We ignore everything else initially.

Hours 5-24: Stabilization & Workaround Deployment

Our technical team dives into the database and code. If a workflow is broken and will take days to code properly, we design a manual workaround immediately. If the server is crashing, we scale the infrastructure. We do whatever it takes to keep the business moving while we investigate the root cause.

Hours 25-48: Root Cause Patching & Verification

Once the business is breathing, we apply targeted patches to the actual code or configuration that caused the failure. We test the fix rigorously and remove the temporary workarounds.
