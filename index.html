Five root causesWhat's actually broken

1. AI Overview cannibalization

3 of 4 highest-impression pages are blog posts ranking pos 9-15 with zero clicks. Listicle and comparison content gets summarized by Google AI Overviews. Users get the answer without clicking through. Pattern signature: best-erp-systems-39 has 1,744 impressions at pos 9.7 and zero clicks.

2. Commercial pages stranded

Every high-intent commercial query (hire odoo developer, odoo consulting services, odoo implementation company) ranks between pos 35 and 76. Page 4-7 generates almost no clicks. This is the lead-gen blocker.

3. Indexation hygiene broken

27 × 404 errors, 78 not indexed, RSS feed inside sitemap, noindex URLs in sitemap, 5 redirects in sitemap, phantom Fleet Management nav item generating 404s.

4. Mobile non-functional

Zero clicks on 469 mobile impressions. Position 30.85 mobile vs 18.07 desktop. Either rendering issue, Core Web Vitals failure, or mobile snippet problem. Mobile-first indexing makes this a sitewide ranking suppressor.

5. Trust signals unused

Adatasol is Odoo Ready Partner (entry tier). Competitors are Gold Partners with "500+ projects" claims. Footer says "Odoo Certified Ready Partner" but the badge is not linked to the official partner directory. Free signal sitting unused.
